Team Trojan

Team Rules and Regulations

  • Weekly Attendance

    • Please make an effort to join the team every Sunday without fail.

  • Leave Application

    • If you can't make it on Sunday, kindly apply for leave by Wednesday night. We understand that life happens, but exceptions will only be made for health issues, welfare issues, family matters, urgent business needs, and exams.

  • Unauthorized Absence

    • In the case of unauthorized absence, a mandatory fine of 10,000 MMK must be paid. This helps us maintain our self-help club.

  • Jersey and Field Fees

    • As this is a self-help club, we appreciate members covering the full cost of jerseys and their proportionate share of field fees.

  • Punctuality

    • Please be at the field before the scheduled time. If you're more than 15 minutes late, a fine of 5,000 MMK will be levied.

  • Monthly Schedule

    • The team typically plays four days a month. Of those, two are for training, and the other two are for matches.

  • Respect in Training

    • Kindly show respect during training. If you miss a meal, you may be fined 10,000 MMK.

  • No Fights

    • There should be absolutely no fighting between team members or with other teams.

  • Rewards for Compliance

    • Any member who fully complies with the above rules will be eligible for rewards from the fines received every 6 months. Your commitment is truly appreciated!
